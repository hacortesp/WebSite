Henry Andrés Cortés Páez¶

About¶

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Basque Center for Applied Mathematics (BCAM), working in the Modelling and Simulation in Life and Material Sciences (MSLMS) group.

My research focuses on:

  • Multiscale modeling of battery materials

  • Solid-state electrolytes

  • Force-field development

  • Molecular dynamics and first-principles simulations

I obtained my PhD in Science and Technology (Physics) from the National University of General San Martín (Argentina), working at CNEA.
